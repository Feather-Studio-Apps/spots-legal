Spots.
Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 28, 2026
The short version: Spots. is a local-first app. Your data lives on your device. We don't collect it, sell it, or send it anywhere.

Who we are

Spots. is developed by Daren Irlen, operating under Feather Studio. If you have any questions, contact us at featherstudio@proton.me.

What data Spots. collects

Spots. does not collect any personal data. The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Places you save (name, address, coordinates, notes, tags, ratings)
  • Photos you attach to spots
  • Visit logs you create
  • Lists you create
  • Your profile name and avatar photo
  • Your in-app preferences (theme, map provider)

None of this data is transmitted to any server, collected by Feather Studio, or shared with any third party.

No account required

Spots. requires no account, no sign-in, and no email address. You can use the app entirely anonymously.

Third-party services

Spots. uses Apple MapKit to display maps and search for places. When you search for a location, your search query is sent to Apple's servers in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. Feather Studio has no access to this data.

When using the Share Extension to save places from other apps, any place search is handled via Apple MapKit on-device. No data is transmitted to Feather Studio.

Spots. does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

iCloud

iCloud sync is not available in the current version of Spots. and is planned for a future update. When available, it will be optional. Any data stored in iCloud is governed by Apple's iCloud terms, and Feather Studio will not have access to it.

Data export & deletion

You can export all your data at any time from the Profile page. You can delete all your data from the Profile page using "Delete All Data." Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data from your device.

Children

Spots. is not directed at children under 13. We do not collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
