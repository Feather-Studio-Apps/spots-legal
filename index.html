What Spots. does

Everything in one place.
Nothing you don't need.

📍
Save any place, instantly

Search from Apple Maps or add manually with an address or pin drop. If you can find it, you can save it.

📲
Share from any app

See a spot on Instagram or Safari? Use the share sheet to save it to Spots. without leaving the app.

✅
Track where you've been

Mark spots as Want, Been, Love, or Skip. Log visits with dates and notes. Upload your own photos.

🗂️
Organise your way

Create lists for trips, neighbourhoods, or occasions. Add tags, ratings, and custom categories.

🗺️
See it on a map

Browse your saved spots on a full map view. Filter by status, category, or list — then get directions in one tap.

⚡
Always ready

When a friend asks where to eat, you have the answer. Your lists are always on you, always up to date.

Your data belongs to you.

No account required. No ads. No algorithm. Everything you save lives on your device — Feather Studio never sees it.
