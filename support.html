Organising
Lists let you group spots however you like — by trip, neighbourhood, occasion, or anything else. A spot can belong to multiple lists. Create and manage lists from Profile → Lists & Tags.
Tags are freeform labels you add to individual spots. Type a tag directly in the spot detail view. Great for things like #patio, #datenight, or #weekend. Manage tags from Profile → Lists & Tags.
Use the filter pills at the bottom of the List and Map tabs to quickly filter by status. Tap the ⊞ icon on the left to open the full filter sheet — where you can filter by status, category, list, tag, and rating, and change the sort order.
Yes. Go to Profile → Lists & Tags, switch to the Lists or Tags tab, and use the up/down arrows on each row to reorder. The order here matches the order they appear in the filter sheet.
Your Data
Everything is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your data may be included in your iCloud backup automatically.
Go to Profile → Export Data. This creates a JSON file with all your spots and visits that you can save anywhere — Files, iCloud Drive, email to yourself, etc.
Export your data from the old phone (Profile → Export Data), then import it on the new phone (Profile → Import Data). Note: importing replaces all existing data on the new device.
Go to Profile → Delete All Data. You'll be asked to confirm before anything is deleted. This is permanent and cannot be undone.
Settings
Go to Profile → Appearance → Theme. Choose Light, Dark, or System (follows your device setting).
Go to Profile → Data → Map Provider. Toggle between Apple Maps and Google Maps. Google Maps must be installed on your device for the Google Maps option to work.
